Boat Ride Builder

About

Boat Ride Builder lets you create your own boat ride adventures from scratch. Lay down winding water tracks, fill your world with colorful scenery and characters, then hop in the boat and enjoy the ride! Perfect for kids and anyone who loves creative building games.
