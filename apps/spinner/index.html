Decision Spin

Can't Decide? Let the Wheel Decide for You!

About

Having trouble making up your mind? Let the wheel decide for you! Decision Spin is the ultimate decision-making tool that lets you create, customize, and spin your own wheels. Whether you are settling a bet, choosing where to eat, or picking a winner for a raffle, our app makes the process easy and exciting.

Stop wasting time debating options. Just enter your choices, customize the spinner to look exactly how you want, and give it a spin!

Key Features

🎨 Fully Customizable

You are in control. Edit the text, choose your favorite colors, and name your spinners with custom titles.

♾️ Create Unlimited Spinners

Make a specific wheel for "Dinner Ideas," another for "Board Game Dice," and another for "Party Dares." Save them all!

⚙️ Smooth Physics

Enjoy a satisfying spinning animation that lands on a random result every time.

✨ Easy to Use

A clean, simple interface that lets you create a new wheel in seconds.

Ways to Use Decision Spin

🤔 Make Decisions

Can't choose between Pizza, Sushi, or Burgers? Put them on the wheel and spin!

🎲 Gaming & RPGs

Lost your dice or spinner? Create a custom wheel with numbers or actions for your board games and D&D sessions.

🏫 Classroom & Education

Teachers can use it to pick a student at random or choose the next activity.

🏆 Raffles & Giveaways

Enter names and spin to pick a fair and random winner.

🏋️ Workout Roulette

Create a wheel with different exercises (Pushups, Squats, Planks) and let the app dictate your workout.

🧹 Chore Assigner

Who has to do the dishes tonight? Let the spinner decide!

Why Decision Spin?

Why leave it to chance? (Actually... do leave it to chance!) Spin the wheel and see what it decides. Download Decision Spin today and turn your hard decisions into fun spins.
