About

Stuck in a fitness rut? Same old exercises, day after day? It's time to mix things up. Welcome to ExerSpin!

We replaced boring workout plans with something way more fun: a giant spinner that picks your next exercise FOR YOU. It's random. It's a challenge. It's your new fitness obsession.

Features

🎡 THE MAGIC SPINNER

Your personal workout DJ. One tap and your next exercise is locked and loaded. No more thinking, just doing.

🏆 LEVEL UP WITH POINTS

Every exercise you crush earns you points. Compete against yourself, build your score, and turn your fitness into a high-score game!

💥 ENDLESS VARIETY

With a huge library of exercises, you'll never get the same workout twice. Keep your body guessing and the results coming.

⚡️ ZERO EXCUSES

Don't know where to start? Just hit spin! ExerSpin is the perfect cure for workout procrastination.
