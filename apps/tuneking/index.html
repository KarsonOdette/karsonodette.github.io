TuneKing

Turn Your Imagination Into Music — In Seconds

About

Unleash your inner producer with TuneKing — the AI music generator that turns a text prompt into a fully realized song in seconds. Pop anthem, lo-fi beat, cinematic score, heavy metal riff — if you can imagine it, TuneKing can make it. No musical experience or equipment required. Join a thriving community of creators, discover incredible sounds from around the world, and put your skills to the test in exciting weekly music contests.

Features

🎶 Text-to-Music Generation

Type a prompt describing your vibe, genre, or lyrics and watch AI bring it to life — complete with vocals, instrumentation, and cover art

🏆 Weekly Contests

A new theme drops every week. Generate your best track, submit it, and climb the leaderboard to claim the title of TuneKing champion

🌍 Global Discover Feed

Stream an endless feed of AI-generated music from creators worldwide — and see the exact prompts they used to achieve their sound

🚀 Easy Sharing

Share your tracks instantly to TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and more — perfect for creators looking for original, royalty-free background music

🎸 Endless Genres

Pop, rap, EDM, rock, country, classical, synth-wave, lo-fi — whatever sound you're chasing, TuneKing can generate it

💎 Credits & Subscriptions

Get started with free credits, then purchase more or subscribe for a weekly stipend to keep the music flowing
