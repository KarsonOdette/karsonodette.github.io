About

Every Hour Executive Block Planner is the intuitive daily planner and reflection tool designed to transform the way you approach each day. Move beyond endless to-do lists into a world of focused action and meaningful progress. Plan your day down to the minute, reflect on your performance, and discover actionable insights to become consistently better. Perfect for executives, professionals, students, and anyone looking to maximize their productivity and well-being.

Features

⏰ Effortless Daily Planning (30-Min Blocks)

Visually map out your entire day in clear, manageable 30-minute increments. From "Deep Work" to "Lunch" to "Client Calls" and "Sleep," know exactly how you intend to spend your precious time

🎯 Prioritize Your Top 3 Goals

Start each day by defining your most important objectives. Ensure your daily plan aligns with what truly matters, keeping your big-picture ambitions front and center

🔔 Real-Time Distraction Tracking

Become hyper-aware of your focus. Tap to log distractions as they happen, giving you invaluable data on when and why your concentration wavers

📝 Daily Rating & Reflection

At the end of each day, give your day a quick 1-10 rating and jot down "What I could have improved." This powerful reflection loop fosters continuous growth

📊 Custom Stats & Self-Quantification

Track anything! Want to hit your daily water intake? Meditate more? Count your positive interactions? Create personalized counters and watch your progress over time

✨ Intuitive & Clean Interface

A beautiful, easy-to-use design makes planning and tracking a joy, not a chore. Reduce decision fatigue and eliminate multitasking to enhance concentration
