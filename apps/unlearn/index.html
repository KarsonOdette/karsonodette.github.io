About

Break free from bad habits and take control of your life with Unlearn — the app designed to help you identify, track, and overcome the behaviors that are holding you back. Whether you're trying to stop nail biting, quit drinking, manage angry outbursts, or break free from procrastination, Unlearn gives you the tools to understand your patterns and make meaningful changes.

Unlearn makes habit tracking simple and intuitive. By logging your habits, you can uncover hidden patterns and see the real impact — how much time you're losing, how much money you're spending, and when you're most vulnerable. The app shows you the hard data so you can start making better choices.

Unlearn isn't about judgment or guilt — it's about building awareness and empowering you to make better choices. Progress isn't always linear, but Unlearn keeps you motivated with real-time insights, encouraging feedback, and milestones to celebrate your success along the way. The goal isn't perfection — it's becoming more aware, more intentional, and more in control of your life.
