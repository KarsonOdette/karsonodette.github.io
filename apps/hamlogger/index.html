Ham Logger

Simple Amateur Radio Contact Logging

About

Ham Logger is a straightforward and efficient logbook for amateur radio operators. Track your QSOs with all essential details including callsign, frequency, mode, RST, and more. Perfect for casual operators and contest logging alike.

Screenshots

Features

🎯 Special Event Support

Full logging support for Field Day, IOTA, POTA, SOTA, WWFF, and Winter Field Day activations

📡 ADIF Import & Export

Seamlessly import and export logs using ADIF 3.1.6 format for easy upload to POTA and other logging services

📍 Automatic Gridsquare via GPS

Automatically calculate your gridsquare using your phone's GPS location

⚡ Clean, Fast Interface

Rapid, error-free logging designed for field operations with minimal taps

♾️ Unlimited Capacity

Store unlimited logbooks and QSOs directly on your device

📝 Comprehensive Data Capture

Log all critical fields: Date, Time, Callsign, Power, Signal Report, Comments, Mode, and Gridsquare

🗺️ Gridsquare Map

Visual map showing your worked gridsquares

📊 QSO Statistics

Detailed breakdowns by band and mode with activity heat graphs

📱 Mobile-First Design

Purpose-built for amateur radio operators in the field with full offline functionality

🔍 Callsign Lookup

Instantly lookup station information by callsign to auto-fill contact details
