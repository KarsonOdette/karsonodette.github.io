US Amateur Band Plan

Quick Reference for Amateur Radio Band Allocations

About

The US Amateur Band Plan app is your essential companion for ham radio operations. Get instant access to all US amateur radio band allocations, modes, and privileges. Perfect for studying, operating, or quick reference in the field.

Screenshots

Features

📻 Complete US Band Plan Reference

Instant access to all US amateur radio bands with frequency allocations sorted by license class (ITU Region 2)

📊 Graphical Band Charts

Visual layouts of each amateur radio band make it easy to find your segment and understand frequency allocations at a glance

🌍 Current Band & RF Conditions

Real-time propagation information with tables and LUF/MUF maps to optimize your operating

🎯 License-Based Band Plans

Tailored displays for Technician, General, Extra, and legacy license classes (Novice and Advanced)

📜 FCC Part 97 Rules

Many band-specific FCC regulations included to keep you compliant with frequency rules and laws

🤝 ARRL Gentleman's Agreements

Voluntary usage suggestions adopted by the ARRL to promote smooth operation and good practice

🏆 Contest & Event Support

Perfect for ARRL Field Day, Parks on the Air (POTA), and seasonal contests when you need fast access to band data in congested conditions

🔍 US Callsign Lookup

Quickly identify hams by their callsign using the built-in lookup with our callsign database

📡 Additional Radio Services

Includes allocated bands/channels for CB, FRS, GMRS, MURS, NOAA Weather Radio, Maritime Radiofacsimile, NIST Time & Frequency signals, and USAF Messages (HFGCS)

🎓 Perfect for License Study

Great learning tool for understanding band privileges while studying for your Technician or General license

📻 SWL Support

Ideal reference for Short Wave Listening enthusiasts monitoring amateur and other radio services

📱 Full Offline Access

Designed to work even in remote locations with no cell service - perfect for field operations, emergency communications, and ham shack use
