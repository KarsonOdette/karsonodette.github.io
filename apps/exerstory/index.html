About

Are you tired of boring routines? ExerStory transforms your daily fitness regime into an immersive workout game. Powered by intelligent AI, ExerStory generates unique, interactive tales where you are the protagonist, and your physical performance determines the plot.

If you have been searching for a motivating workout that actually keeps you engaged, this is it. You aren't just doing push-ups in your living room; you are diving for cover during a bank heist. You aren't just holding a plank; you are hiding in the shadows to avoid detection.

How It Works

1. Choose Your Adventure

Type in any prompt (e.g., "A spy escaping a facility" or "A knight defending the realm") or let the AI surprise you.

2. Read Your Workout Story

The app generates a thrilling narrative segment ending in a cliffhanger.

3. Do Simple Exercises

The story assigns a physical action (e.g., Tactical Crouch = Squats).

4. Shape the Narrative

Success: You complete the reps? Your character succeeds, gaining the upper hand.
Failure: Can't hold the plank? Your character cramps, trips, or gets spotted, forcing the story down a harder path.
