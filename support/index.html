Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Email us at bioruststudios+support@gmail.com with a description of the issue, your device model, and device OS version. Screenshots or screen recordings are always helpful.

How do I request a refund?

Request one at bioruststudios+support@gmail.com. Some refunds you can also request through Google Play or the App Store.

I have a feature request. Where do I send it?

We'd love to hear your ideas! Send feature requests to bioruststudios+support@gmail.com with the app name in the subject line.

How long does it take to receive a response?

We aim to respond to all support emails within 1–3 business days.
